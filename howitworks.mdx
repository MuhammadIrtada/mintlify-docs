---
title: How Verse AI Work
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Input">
    User submits a text prompt (e.g., “A futuristic city at sunset”).
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Processing">
    AI generates either a 3D model **(5 \$VERSE reward)** or video **(10 \$VERSE reward)**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verse AI Museum">
    Content is displayed in the [Verse AI](https://verseai.app/) Metaverse Museum.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore">
    Users can explore, interact, and even license their creations.
  </Step>
</Steps>
